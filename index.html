Typing Guide Features:

Typing Test:

Typing guide has its own typing test where you can discover your rank and speed through either typing random words or quoted texts. Results of each test include raw speed, wpm, accuracy, and time.

Progress Tracker:

Typing guide stores a bunch of information of your tests like speed, accuracy, and date into the local storage so that you can keep track of your progress and see how well you’re improving. The information is displayed through a line graph.

Guiding articles:

Improving the typing speed varies a lot within each level. That’s why Typing Guide has 4 guiding articles in which you can discover tips, advice, and general things you should be doing based on your experience.
